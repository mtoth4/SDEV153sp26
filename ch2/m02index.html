Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Experience the beauty of the coastline from the water. Kayak through serene coves and explore hidden beaches.

Bird Watching

Observe a variety of native bird species in their natural habitat. Join our guided bird watching tours.
